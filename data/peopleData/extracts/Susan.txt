<p><b>Susan</b> is a feminine given name, from French <i>Susanne</i>, from Late Latin <i>Susanna</i>, from Greek <i>Sousanna</i>, from Hebrew <i>Šošanna</i>, literally meaning "lily", a term derived from <i>Susa</i> (Persian: <i>Šuš</i>), a city in southwest Iran that was the ancient capital of the Elamite kingdom and Achaemenid empire.</p>
